Vadym Tyemirov

Technical Founder & Engineering Leader

Vadym Tyemirov is a product-minded engineering leader who bridges the gap between cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence and scalable enterprise infrastructure. With a career spanning three decades at world-class companies including Meta, BlackRock, Shopify, and Morgan Stanley, Vadym specializes in turning complex technical challenges into revenue-generating products.

Previously, Vadym was a Technical Program Manager at Meta, where he successfully scaled critical privacy compliance initiatives and completely redesigned large-scale ML infrastructure to handle growing global user demands. As a Senior Architect at BlackRock, he spearheaded the transition to a high-throughput Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA), successfully modernizing financial asset retrieval for the cloud era and delivering critical system resilience.

Vadym's expertise in Applied AI/ML is rooted in tangible business outcomes:

  • At Shopify, he built recommendation engines that directly drove merchant sales growth.
  • At CrowdFlower, he led the "FlowerAI" initiative, a strategic pivot that transformed the company's business model from micro-tasking to automated ML data analysis.

He holds a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Taurida National University. Having built high-impact recommendation engines and led pivotal AI strategy shifts throughout his career, Vadym is now dedicated to leveraging his deep expertise to leverage AI in product development in an interesting way as a founder.
